About Us

Learn about Aaroha School of Music and the musical journey of its founder, Anurag Naik.

Anurag Naik - Founder & Music Director

With over 15 years of experience in music education and performance, Anurag Naik established Aaroha School of Music with a vision to inspire and nurture musical talent across all age groups.

Trained in both Western and Indian classical traditions, Anurag holds a Master's degree in Music Performance and has collaborated with renowned artists across the globe. His unique teaching methodology combines traditional techniques with modern approaches to create a holistic learning experience.
